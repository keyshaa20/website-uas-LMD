What is Student Mobility Program at UIN Sunan Gunung Djati ?

The student mobility program is a program that allows students to study and experience at other universities abroad. This program is usually carried out with the home campus which already has cooperation. UIN Bandung offers opportunities for students to study abroad. This program is designed to improve the quality of education and open international horizons. UIN Bandung has collaborations with various universities abroad. Through the student exchange program, students can study at partner universities, while remaining registered at UIN Bandung.
